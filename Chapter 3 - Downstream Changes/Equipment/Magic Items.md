### Armour
**_Adamantine Armour_**: Attack rolls made against you have their roll on the d20 reduced by 1. Once per short or long rest, you can activate the _Adamantine Aura_ with a Bonus Action for one minute.
- _Adamantine Aura_: An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. While in the aura, attacks against targets in the aura have their roll on the d20 reduced by 2.

**_Elemental Armour_**: You have an additional Damage Reduction of 5 against a given damage type chosen from Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning and Thunder damage.

**_Scaledrain Armour_**: Once per short or long rest, you can activate the _Scaledrain Aura_ with a Bonus Action for one minute.
- _Scaledrain Aura_: An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. While in the aura, targets have their Damage Reduction reduced by 2. Your Damage Reduction increases by 1 for every target in the aura.
### Prefixes
**_Infused ..._**: This weapon deals an additional 1d4 damage of a given type with attacks made with it.

**_Guiding ..._**: This weapon grants an additional +2 to attack rolls made with it.

**_Silent ..._**: Attacks made with this weapon or piece of ammunition can't be perceived through sound.

**_Wounding ..._**: When this weapon or piece of ammunition is used as part of an Aimed Strike and deals damage, the Wound Save DC equals the whole damage or 15, whichever is higher, instead.
