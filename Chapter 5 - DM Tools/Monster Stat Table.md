### Defence
**_Smalls_**: High Hit DC, at least 14. Damage Reduction should be close to 0. d6 Hit Dice recommended.

**_Shells_**: High Damage Reduction, roughly half CR + 5. Hit DC should not be above 14. d8 Hit Dice recommended.

**_Sponges_**: High Hit Point Total, d12 Hit Dice. Hit DC should be less than 10, Damage Reduction should be close to 0.
