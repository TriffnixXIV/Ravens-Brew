### Small Characters
In accordance with the rules described in [[AC-less Defence]], small characters gain the following benefits:
- They have a Hit DC of 12.
- They have a +2 bonus to attack rolls.

As these are quite potent benefits, the following weaknesses are introduced for small characters:
- They have the damage they deal with weapon attacks decreased by 1 (but never below 1).
- They have their movement speed reduced by 5 feet.
- When entering into a class or gaining a class level, the Hit Die they gain through that class is reduced to the next-lower die.
	- For example, when taking a Wizard level, they get a d4 instead of a d6, and when taking a Barbarian level, they get a d10 instead of a d12.
	- This also reduces the Hit Points they gain through level ups accordingly. They roll the lower die instead, and when taking the average, they gain the average of that lower die instead.
