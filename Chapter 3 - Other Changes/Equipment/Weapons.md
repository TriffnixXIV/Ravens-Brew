**_Weapon Training_**: Similar to other equipment, weapons now work through Training.

**_Proficiency Conversion_**: Features that previously gave Proficiency with one or more weapons now provides Training with those weapons instead. When a feature previously required Proficiency with a weapon, it now requires Training with this weapon instead.

For example, you no longer start with Proficiency with the weapons listed in your class description. Instead, you have Training with those weapons.

**_Weapon Attacks_**: When you attack with a weapon you aren't trained with, you make the attack roll with Disadvantage. You add your ability modifier to attack rolls as usual, but you don't add your Proficiency Bonus to attacks made with weapons you are trained with.

When you receive [[Martial Training]], you can choose the Accuracy feature to add your Proficiency Bonus to certain attack rolls again.

**_Weapon Mastery Properties_**: Weapons no longer have Weapon Mastery Properties, as those have been turned into [[Martial Training]] features. When you previously gained access to a Weapon Mastery Property, you now receive Martial Training instead.
