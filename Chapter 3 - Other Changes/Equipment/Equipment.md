[[Armour|Armour]] and [[Shields]] are changed in accordance with [[AC-less Defence]]. Additionally, [[Armour#Armour Pieces|Armour Pieces]] are introduced to add more variance and to protect against [[Aimed Strikes]], and new [[Shields]] are introduced to provide distinct interactions with [[AC-less Defence|Cover]] and [[Blocking]].

**_Training_**: All equipment, including [[Weapons|weapons]], [[Armour|armour]], [[Shields|shields]], [[Tools|tools]] and [[Spell Scrolls|scrolls]], work solely through Training now. Proficiency and Expertise are reserved for skill checks that can be used with equipment.

When using equipment you lack Training with, you have Disadvantage on any D20 Test that relies on the use of that equipment.
